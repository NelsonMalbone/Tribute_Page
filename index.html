Basic Info

Adam Whitney Savage An american special effect designer and fabricator, actor, educator and television producer co hosted with Jamie Hyneman on tv show called Mythbusters


Early-Life

Adam was born is New York City and was rasied in Sleepy Hollow, New York. Begun to act around the age of 5, stopped acting around the age of 19. "he passed on that in favor of doing stuff with my hands" Described Mythbusters as "the perfect marriage of the two things" November 25 2011 he recieved an honorary doctorate from the University of Twente (Enschede Netherlands)


Career

Adam Has had many different careers paths from being an Animator, graphic designer, carpenter, projectionist, film developer, television presenter, set designer, toy designer and a gallery owner. Adam has worked as a model maker on films such as galaxy quest, stars wars episode II attack of the clones, the mummy, the matrix reloaded and many more. he taught previously taught advance model at the department of Industrial Design at Academy of Art Unversity in Sans Fransico
